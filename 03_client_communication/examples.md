## EXAMPLE 1 — Buyer Welcome Email (Operational Mode / Elena)

**Situation:** Deal #412-buyer confirmed. Chens are relocating from Chicago. Referral from the Nguyen family. Elena's first outreach.

**Agent profile:** Elena — warm-professional, measured pace, sign-off "Talk soon, Elena"

---

**Draft:**

```
Subject: Welcome — and a few things to know before we start

Hi Robert and Maria,

I'm so glad the Nguyens connected us. I've had the chance to look over
what you're looking for, and I think we're going to find something
really good together.

Travis Heights and South Congress are two of my favorite parts of Austin
to work in — walkable, full of character, and genuinely different from
what you'll find in most cities. I think you'll understand why quickly.

A few things from my end: I've already pulled a market brief on both
neighborhoods so we're starting with a clear picture of what's out there
at your range. I'll send that over shortly. And once you have a moment,
I'd love to do a quick call to make sure I have your priorities exactly
right before we start scheduling anything.

The 60-day timeline is workable. We'll want to move with some intention
but there's no need to rush into anything that doesn't feel right.

Let me know if you have any questions — I'm always reachable.

Talk soon, Elena
```

## EXAMPLE 2 — Competing Offer (Operational Mode / Elena)

**Situation:** Deal #412-buyer. The Chens' first-choice property at 2219 Bluebonnet received a competing offer. Deadline to respond is 8pm tonight.

---

**Draft:**

```
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
OPTION A — Create Urgency
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
Subject: Update on Bluebonnet — We Need to Talk Today

Hi Robert and Maria,

I wanted to reach out right away. The sellers have let us know another
offer has come in on 2219 Bluebonnet, and we have until 8pm tonight to
respond.

I know this is a lot to process quickly. I have a clear picture of what
our options are, and I think we're in a strong position to compete if
this is the home you want. I'd love to connect in the next hour so we
can talk through it.

I'm available now.

Talk soon, Elena

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
OPTION B — Measured and Informative
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
Subject: Important Update — 2219 Bluebonnet Lane

Hi Robert and Maria,

I have an update I wanted to share with you directly. The sellers have
received another offer on the property, and we have a response deadline
of 8pm this evening.

I want to walk you through what this means and what your options are —
there's no pressure to do anything other than make the decision that's
right for you. Let's talk today so we have time to be thoughtful about it.

Let me know if you have any questions — I'm always reachable.

Talk soon, Elena

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
Option A creates urgency and positions Elena as ready to move.
Option B is measured — gives the Chens space and positions Elena as their advisor.
Choose based on how motivated the Chens have signaled they are about this property.
```

## EXAMPLE 3 — Inspection Issue (Guided Mode / Marco)

**Situation:** Deal #412-buyer. Inspection on 2219 Bluebonnet came back with roof issues. Seller offered an $8,000 credit. Inspection deadline is tomorrow at 5pm. Marco needs to present options to the Chens.

---

**Draft + Coaching Note:**

```
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
DRAFT — Inspection Update
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
Subject: Inspection Update — 2219 Bluebonnet

Hi Robert and Maria,

The inspection report came back on 2219 Bluebonnet and I wanted to
walk you through what it found and where things stand.

The inspector noted issues with the roof — specifically [add specific
finding from inspection report]. The sellers have responded by offering
an $8,000 credit at closing, which would give you funds to address the
repair after you take ownership.

You have three options from here:

1. Accept the $8,000 credit and proceed — the credit offsets the repair
   cost and keeps the deal on track

2. Request additional compensation or a repair — we go back to the
   sellers and negotiate further before the deadline

3. Terminate the contract — your inspection contingency gives you the
   right to walk away before tomorrow at 5pm with your earnest money
   returned

I want to make sure you have everything you need to make the decision
that's right for you. Can we connect today? The deadline is tomorrow
at 5pm, so we'll want to confirm your direction before then.

Talk soon, Marco

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
COACHING NOTE FOR MARCO
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
Before sending: fill in the specific roof finding from the inspection
report where marked. "Roof issues" is too vague — clients need the
specific detail to make an informed decision.

Tone note: This draft leads with the facts and gives three clear options.
That structure works well for inspection conversations — clients often
feel overwhelmed, and clear options reduce that anxiety.

What to watch for in their reply: If they ask "what would you do?"
that is your cue to share your professional opinion — not before.
Let them ask first. Offering your opinion unprompted on terminate vs.
proceed can create liability if the deal goes wrong later.

After they decide: come back and I'll draft the formal inspection
response letter for their chosen direction.
```

— Earnest money: due within [earnest_money_due_date]

— Appraisal: your lender will order this, typically within the first week

Talk soon, Elena

## EXAMPLE 5 — Closing Day Prep (Operational Mode / Carlos)

**Situation:** Deal #327-seller. Hoffman property closing is tomorrow.

**Agent profile:** Carlos — formal-professional, deliberate, sign-off "Best, Carlos"

---

**Draft:**

```
Subject: Closing Tomorrow — Everything You Need to Know

Patricia and James,

Tomorrow is the day. Here is everything you need for a smooth closing.

CLOSING DETAILS
Time: [closing_time]
Location: [escrow_company_address]
What to bring: Government-issued photo ID for both of you

BEFORE YOU ARRIVE
— Complete your final walkthrough of the property if you haven't already
— Ensure the property is cleared out and in the agreed condition
— Collect all keys, garage remotes, and access codes — you'll hand
  those over at closing

WHAT HAPPENS AT CLOSING
You'll sign the deed transferring ownership and the settlement statement
confirming all financial terms. Once documents are signed and funds are
confirmed, the deed will be recorded with the county — at that point,
the sale is complete.

AFTER CLOSING
Your proceeds will be wired to the account on file with the title company,
typically within 24–48 hours of recording.

It has been a pleasure working with you on this. I'll be there tomorrow
and will make sure everything goes smoothly.

Best, Carlos
```

## EXAMPLE 7 — Cold Reengagement (Guided Mode / Marco)

**Situation:** Deal #089-buyer. David Lara went cold 90 days ago. His original 90-day timeline is still technically live. System triggers reengagement suggestion.

---

**Draft + Coaching Note:**

```
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
DRAFT — Reengagement
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
Subject: Checking In — East Austin

Hi David,

It's been a little while and I wanted to check in. The East Austin
market has shifted in the last few months — there's more inventory
and buyers have more room to negotiate than they did when we last spoke.

No pressure at all — just wanted to make sure you had a current picture
if the timing ever feels right to revisit things.

How are you doing?

Talk soon, Marco

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
COACHING NOTE FOR MARCO
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
This draft is intentionally light. The goal is a reply — not a
commitment. If David responds at all, that is a win.

The market update gives him a real reason to re-engage that isn't
just "are you still looking?" The final line "How are you doing?"
is personal and opens the door without asking about real estate.

If he replies: come back and I'll draft your follow-up based on
what he says. Do not send a property list in the first reply —
wait until he re-engages before pushing into search mode.
```
