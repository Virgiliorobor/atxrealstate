## NEIGHBORHOOD IDENTITY

**Character:** Nature-forward luxury. Premier South Austin destination for high-net-worth buyers prioritizing privacy, outdoor access, and top-tier schools without sacrificing downtown proximity. Established homes, mature trees, significant architectural character.

**Location:** South Austin. Immediate access to Zilker Park and Barton Creek Greenbelt. 3.5 miles from downtown.

**Lifestyle:** Greenbelt hiking, swimming, rock climbing minutes from home. Car-dependent for most errands. Quiet residential streets — not a social scene neighborhood.

**Environmental Identity:** Protected from over-development by the Save Our Springs Ordinance — limits impervious cover to 15–25%. Keeps the neighborhood's natural character intact but restricts what can be built.

**Critical Distinction:** Barton Hills (inner core, 78704) vs. Barton Creek (78735) are different markets. Eanes ISD zoning varies by specific address within the same neighborhood — always verify.

## SCHOOLS — PRIMARY VALUE DRIVER

**This is the single most important section for Barton Hills buyer clients.**

### Eanes ISD (Primary value driver — properties in this district command significant premium)
- Ranked **#1 in Texas, #7 nationally** (2026)
- Families consistently target Barton Creek specifically for this district
- **Critical warning:** AISD and Eanes ISD boundaries can fluctuate within the same neighborhood and even the same street. Always verify exact address zoning before making school-based recommendations.

### Barton Hills Elementary (AISD — for properties not in Eanes ISD)
- GreatSchools: 8/10 | Niche: A-
- Enrollment: 303 students — small campus, highly personalized environment
- Solid school but not the Eanes ISD premium driver

**Agent protocol:** For any client where school district is a factor, verify the specific property address against the AISD/Eanes boundary map before showing. A property one street over from Eanes zoning is a materially different purchase.

## ENVIRONMENTAL RISKS — MUST DISCLOSE

Barton Hills faces significant climate risks buyers are increasingly factoring into valuations. These must be raised proactively with clients:

**Flooding:** ~12% of properties face severe flood risk over the next 30 years
**Wildfire:** ~13% of properties face moderate wildfire risk over the next 30 years

Environmental risks are increasingly impacting:
- Insurance premiums (rising significantly)
- Buyer valuation models
- Lender requirements for certain properties

**Agent protocol:** For any Barton Hills property, pull flood and fire risk data for the specific address early in the process. Do not wait for buyers to discover this at inspection.

## HOUSING STOCK

- Established single-family homes with significant character and mature oak trees
- Architectural variety: ranch style, mid-century modern, contemporary
- Lot sizes typically larger than other central Austin neighborhoods
- Save Our Springs Ordinance limits new construction density
- Condo segment exists at entry price points within the neighborhood
- Teardown/rebuild activity limited by ordinance — existing stock matters

## STRATEGIC NOTES FOR DIANA'S TEAM

**Buyer strategy at $600k–$950k (Diana's typical range):**
Single-family access at this price range is tight. Look at Barton Creek (78735) where 18.1% correction and 9-10% below list negotiability opens options. Barton Hills inner core single-family starts well above $1M. Condos exist at entry level but that is a different purchase thesis.

**Buyer strategy for school-focused clients:**
Eanes ISD verification is the first step — before showing any property. A non-Eanes address in Barton Hills is a fundamentally different value proposition. Do not make school assumptions from neighborhood name alone.

**Seller strategy:**
Nature access and Eanes ISD are the two strongest narratives. Environmental risk disclosure is required — proactive disclosure with mitigation context is better than reactive discovery at inspection. Pricing at 90-93% of list is realistic — buyers are getting 7-10% off and expecting it.

**Strategic recommendation (per research):**
Verify Eanes ISD zoning for maximum resale value. Properties with Eanes zoning consistently outperform in resale.
