## NEIGHBORHOOD IDENTITY

**Character:** Historic, walkable, culturally significant. Gold Standard urban Austin lifestyle south of Lady Bird Lake. Blend of early 20th-century homes and modern infill. One of Austin's most resilient neighborhoods by baseline value.

**Location:** Immediately south of Lady Bird Lake, east of South Congress (SoCo) corridor. 1.8 miles from downtown Austin.

**Walk Score:** 78 | **Bike Score:** 88
**Lifestyle:** Car-light genuinely feasible. Walkable to South Congress restaurants, music venues, and boutiques.

**Natural Environment:** Direct access to Ann and Roy Butler Hike-and-Bike Trail, Stacy Park, Blunn Creek Preserve.

**Trade-offs:** Weekend foot traffic and parking pressure from South Congress proximity. Not suitable for buyers who want quiet suburban streets.

**Elementary:** Travis Heights Elementary (AISD)
- Niche Grade: B+
- Ranked #14 Best Charter Elementary in Austin Area
- Enrollment: ~460 students, grades PK–5
- Student-teacher ratio: 12:1

**Feeder Path:** Travis Heights Elementary → Lively Middle School → Austin High School
- Austin High School: A Niche grade, 7/10 GreatSchools rating

**School Assessment for Clients:**
Good schools — not the primary driver buyers choose this neighborhood for. Buyers here are choosing lifestyle and location. School-focused families often compare to Barton Hills / Eanes ISD.

## HOUSING STOCK

- Mix of early 20th-century bungalows and craftsman homes alongside modern infill
- Many original properties offer renovation opportunity — priced lower, higher upside
- Modern builds command significant premium ($900k+)
- Condo segment exists at lower price points (see East Side Dr comp)
- Lot sizes typically small — urban neighborhood, not suburban space

## STRATEGIC NOTES FOR DIANA'S TEAM

**Buyer strategy:**
Target unrenovated bungalows for value-add opportunity. Correctly priced homes at $600k–$800k are realistic for buyers with those budgets. Do not overpromise — this is not a $500k neighborhood for 3br/2ba with modern finishes.

**Seller strategy:**
Pricing discipline is critical — 53.5% failure rate is the highest risk. Price at or slightly below comparable sales. Sellers anchored to 2022 peak values will sit. Boutique presentation and professional photography matter in this market.

**For relocating buyers (Chen profile):**
Excellent match for out-of-state buyers wanting walkable urban Austin lifestyle with character. Manage expectations on size vs. price — Chicago buyers often expect more square footage per dollar.
