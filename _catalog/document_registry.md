# **US Residential Real Estate Transaction: Document Master List**

This guide covers the standard documentation required for a mid-range residential transaction, tailored for a boutique firm's operational workflow.

## **Phase 1: Pre-Listing & Agency (The Prep Stage)**

*Goal: Legally establish the relationship and prepare the property for market.*

| Document | Purpose | Primary Responsibility |
| :---- | :---- | :---- |
| **Agency Disclosure** | Explains the legal duties of a real estate agent to the client. | Real Estate Firm |
| **Exclusive Right to Sell Agreement** | The contract giving the firm the right to market the home and earn a commission. | Seller & Firm |
| **Wire Fraud Advisory** | Critical notice warning clients about phishing scams regarding closing funds. | Firm (to Client) |
| **Listing Input Sheet** | Internal document containing all home specs for the MLS entry. | Firm |
| **Affiliated Business Disclosure** | Discloses if the firm has financial interests in suggested title or mortgage companies. | Firm |

## **Phase 2: Mandatory Seller Disclosures**

*Goal: Transparency regarding the home's condition to protect against future lawsuits.*

| Document | Purpose | Primary Responsibility |
| :---- | :---- | :---- |
| **Seller’s Property Disclosure (SPD)** | A comprehensive form where the seller lists known defects or history of the home. | Seller |
| **Lead-Based Paint Disclosure** | Federally mandated for any home built before 1978\. | Seller |
| **Natural Hazard Disclosure (NHD)** | Report showing if the home is in a flood, fire, or seismic zone. | Third-Party Provider |
| **Preliminary Title Report** | Shows the legal ownership history and any liens/encumbrances on the property. | Title Company |
| **HOA Documents (if applicable)** | Includes CC\&Rs, bylaws, and meeting minutes for planned communities. | HOA / Seller |

## **Phase 3: The Offer & Contract (Under Contract)**

*Goal: Define the terms of the sale and secure the "Good Faith" deposit.*

| Document | Purpose | Primary Responsibility |
| :---- | :---- | :---- |
| **Residential Purchase Agreement (RPA)** | The main contract detailing price, dates, and contingencies. | Buyer Agent |
| **Pre-Approval Letter** | Verification from a lender that the buyer can afford the home. | Buyer / Lender |
| **Proof of Funds (POF)** | Bank statements showing the buyer has the cash for the down payment. | Buyer |
| **Earnest Money Receipt** | Proof that the buyer deposited the initial funds into an escrow account. | Escrow/Title Co |
| **Counter Offer(s)** | Legal addendums used to negotiate specific terms of the original offer. | Seller/Buyer |

## **Phase 4: Due Diligence & Financing**

*Goal: Verify the home’s value and condition before the "point of no return."*

| Document | Purpose | Primary Responsibility |
| :---- | :---- | :---- |
| **Home Inspection Report** | A detailed analysis of the home's structure and systems. | Buyer (ordered) |
| **Appraisal Report** | A professional valuation required by the lender to justify the loan amount. | Lender |
| **Request for Repair (RR)** | The buyer’s list of items they want fixed or credited before closing. | Buyer |
| **Contingency Removal (CR)** | The most critical document where the buyer formally waives their right to cancel. | Buyer |
| **Loan Estimate (LE)** | A standard form provided by the lender detailing estimated loan costs. | Lender |

## **Phase 5: Closing & Transfer (The Finish Line)**

*Goal: Finalize the finances and legally transfer the title.*

| Document | Purpose | Primary Responsibility |
| :---- | :---- | :---- |
| **Closing Disclosure (CD)** | The final statement of all costs for the buyer and seller. | Lender / Escrow |
| **ALTA Settlement Statement** | An itemized list of all debits and credits for the transaction. | Settlement Agent |
| **Warranty Deed / Grant Deed** | The document that legally transfers the property from seller to buyer. | Seller (signed) |
| **Promissory Note** | The buyer's legal "I.O.U." to the lender. | Buyer |
| **Deed of Trust (Mortgage)** | The document that secures the loan against the property. | Buyer |
| **1099-S Form** | Tax form used to report the proceeds of the real estate sale to the IRS. | Settlement Agent |
| **Final Walkthrough Attestation** | Confirmation that the home is in the agreed-upon condition before funds release. | Buyer |
